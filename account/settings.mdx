Every preference that shapes how NineStyle looks, feels, and communicates with you lives in one place: the **Account Settings** page. Reach it at any time by selecting your avatar in the top-right corner of NineStyle and choosing **Settings** from the dropdown. The settings sidebar groups options into focused sections — Profile, Notifications, Security, Display, and Connected Apps — so you can find and update what you need without hunting through menus.

## Profile settings

Your profile is your NineStyle identity — it's what other members see when they browse the community feed or visit your style page.

<Accordion title="Name">
  Update your display name by selecting the **Name** field, typing your preferred name, and selecting **Save Changes**. Your display name appears on your public profile, outfit posts, and comments.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Email address">
  Change the email address associated with your account under **Profile → Email**. After saving, NineStyle sends a confirmation link to your new address. Your email change does not take effect until you confirm it. Until then, your original address remains active.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Profile photo (avatar)">
  Upload a new avatar by selecting your current photo and choosing **Upload New Photo**. NineStyle accepts JPEG, PNG, and WebP images up to 5 MB. Images are automatically cropped to a circle at 400 × 400 px.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bio">
  Add a short bio (up to 160 characters) that describes your personal style or how you use NineStyle. Your bio appears beneath your name on your public profile page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Location">
  Set your city or region to help NineStyle tailor recommendations for your local climate and seasonal trends. Location is displayed on your profile only if you choose to make it visible in your **Privacy settings**.
</Accordion>

## Notification preferences

Control exactly which notifications NineStyle sends and how it sends them. Go to **Settings → Notifications** to manage each option.

| Notification | Channels | Default |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily recommendation digest** | Email, Push | On (Push only) |
| **New follower alerts** | Email, Push | On (Push only) |
| **Weekly wardrobe analytics report** | Email | On |
| **Promotional emails** | Email | On (opt-out available) |

- **Daily recommendation digest** — Receive your AI-powered outfit suggestions for the day each morning. Choose email, push notification, or both, and set your preferred delivery time in the **Delivery Time** field.
- **New follower alerts** — Get notified when someone follows your NineStyle profile. Turn this off if you prefer to check followers manually from your profile page.
- **Weekly wardrobe analytics report** — Receive a summary of your most-worn items, cost-per-wear insights, and style trends every Monday morning. This is an email-only notification.
- **Promotional emails** — Occasional emails about new NineStyle features, partner offers, and style guides. Select **Opt Out** to stop receiving these while keeping all other email notifications active.

## Security settings

**Changing your password**
Go to **Settings → Security → Change Password**. Enter your current password, then type and confirm your new password. Passwords must be at least 10 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. Select **Update Password** to save. NineStyle immediately invalidates all other active sessions when you change your password — you will need to log in again on other devices.

**Enabling two-factor authentication (2FA)**
Two-factor authentication adds a second verification step each time you log in, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Authenticator app (recommended)">
    1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
    2. Select **Set Up Authenticator App**.
    3. Open your authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password) and scan the QR code displayed on screen.
    4. Enter the 6-digit code generated by the app to verify the connection.
    5. Save your **backup codes** in a secure location — these allow you to access your account if you lose your device.
    6. Select **Enable 2FA** to activate.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="SMS">
    1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
    2. Select **Set Up SMS Authentication**.
    3. Enter your mobile phone number, including country code, and select **Send Code**.
    4. Enter the 6-digit code sent to your phone to verify the number.
    5. Select **Enable 2FA** to activate.

    <Note>
      SMS authentication is less secure than an authenticator app because SMS messages can be intercepted. We recommend using an authenticator app whenever possible.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Display preferences

Go to **Settings → Display** to customize how NineStyle looks and what units it uses.

- **Language** — Choose from over 20 supported languages. Changing the language updates all NineStyle interface text immediately; community content posted by other users is not translated.
- **Currency** — Set your preferred currency for price displays in shopping recommendations and cost-per-wear calculations. Prices are converted at daily exchange rates.
- **Measurement units** — Switch between **Metric** (centimeters, kilograms) and **Imperial** (inches, pounds) for sizing and body measurement fields.
- **Dark mode** — Toggle dark mode on or off. NineStyle also respects your device's system-level dark mode setting by default; turn this toggle off if you want to override the system preference.

<Tip>
  Enable two-factor authentication now — it takes less than two minutes and is the single most effective step you can take to protect your NineStyle account and the personal style data stored within it. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication** to get started.
</Tip>
