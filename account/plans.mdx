NineStyle is free to get started and grows with your wardrobe. The **Free plan** gives you everything you need to build your virtual wardrobe and explore the basics of AI-powered styling, while **Pro** unlocks the full NineStyle experience — unlimited items, advanced recommendations, shopping and social integrations, and an outfit calendar that keeps your looks organized day by day. Choose the plan that fits where you are right now; you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

## Plan comparison

<Tabs>

## Upgrading to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Account menu">
    Select your avatar in the top-right corner of any NineStyle page and choose **Account** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    In the left sidebar, select **Billing**. Your current plan is displayed at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Upgrade to Pro">
    Select the **Upgrade to Pro** button to open the plan selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your billing cycle">
    Select either **Monthly ($9.99/month)** or **Annual ($79.99/year)**. The annual plan is highlighted with projected savings. You can switch billing cycles at any time from the Billing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your payment details">
    Enter your credit or debit card information. NineStyle uses Stripe for secure payment processing — your card details are never stored on NineStyle servers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your upgrade">
    Review your order summary and select **Confirm Upgrade**. Your Pro features are activated immediately. You will receive a confirmation email with your receipt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your subscription

**Canceling Pro**
You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from **Account → Billing → Manage Subscription → Cancel Plan**. Canceling stops future charges and downgrades your account to Free at the end of your current billing period. Your data and wardrobe remain intact on the Free plan, subject to the 50-item limit.

**Pausing your subscription**
If you need a break, you can pause your Pro subscription for up to 3 months rather than canceling. While paused, your account reverts to Free features but your Pro data — integrations, recommendations history, and analytics — is preserved and reactivated automatically when your pause period ends. To pause, go to **Account → Billing → Manage Subscription → Pause Plan**.

**Changing your billing cycle**
Switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from **Account → Billing → Manage Subscription → Change Billing Cycle**. Switching to annual billing applies a pro-rated credit for any unused days on your current monthly cycle.

**Refund policy**
NineStyle offers pro-rated refunds for annual subscriptions canceled within 30 days of purchase or renewal. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period has begun. To request a refund, contact [support@ninestyle.com](mailto:support@ninestyle.com) with your account email and the reason for your request.
