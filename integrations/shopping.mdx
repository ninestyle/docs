Shopping integrations turn NineStyle into a living record of everything you own and everything you want. When you connect a retailer, NineStyle automatically imports your confirmed purchases into your virtual wardrobe, uses your order history to sharpen its AI recommendations, and surfaces new arrivals that match your established style profile. Instead of manually photographing every new item, your wardrobe stays up to date the moment your order is confirmed.

## Supported retailers

NineStyle currently supports direct integration with the following fashion retailers:

- **ASOS**
- **Zara**
- **H&M**
- **UNIQLO**
- **Amazon Fashion**
- **Nordstrom**
- **NET-A-PORTER**
- **Farfetch**

<Note>
  New retail partners are added on a rolling basis. Check the **Connected Apps** page in your settings for the latest list of supported retailers. If your preferred retailer is not yet supported, you can vote for it in the [NineStyle Feature Request portal](https://feedback.ninestyle.com).
</Note>

## Connecting a retailer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Select your avatar in the top-right corner of NineStyle and choose **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Connected Apps">
    In the left sidebar, select **Connected Apps**, then choose the **Shopping** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a retailer">
    Browse the list of supported retailers and select **Connect** next to the one you want to link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize NineStyle">
    You will be redirected to the retailer's website. Log in to your retailer account if prompted, then review the permission request and select **Authorize** or **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    You are returned to NineStyle. The retailer now appears as **Connected** in your Connected Apps list. NineStyle begins importing your purchase history in the background — this may take a few minutes for large order histories.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What NineStyle does with your shopping data

Understanding how your data is used helps you get the most from shopping integrations while staying in control.

**Auto-imports purchases into your wardrobe**
Every confirmed order from a connected retailer is automatically added to your virtual wardrobe, complete with product images, color, size, and category metadata pulled directly from the retailer's product catalog.

**Improves your recommendations**
NineStyle's AI analyzes your purchase history — the brands you buy, the styles you return to, the price points you prefer — to make outfit suggestions and new-item recommendations more relevant to your actual taste, not just browsing behavior.

**Never shares your data with third parties**
Your shopping data belongs to you. NineStyle does not sell, license, or share your order history or wardrobe contents with any third party, including the retailers you connect. Data flows from retailers into NineStyle only — never in the other direction. See the [Privacy Policy](https://ninestyle.com/privacy) for full details.

## Wishlists and saved items

In addition to confirmed purchases, you can save items you're considering buying using the **NineStyle Browser Extension** for Chrome and Firefox. While browsing any supported retailer's product page, select the NineStyle extension icon and choose **Save to Wishlist** or **Add to Wardrobe**. Saved items appear immediately in your NineStyle Wishlist, where you can organize them by collection, compare outfit combinations, and get notified when prices drop.

<Tip>
  Install the NineStyle Browser Extension to save items from any retailer — even those not yet officially integrated. The extension works on thousands of fashion websites and lets you clip product images, prices, and links directly into your wardrobe or wishlist without leaving the retailer's page. Download it from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore) or [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org).
</Tip>
