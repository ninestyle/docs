Your social feeds are already full of style ideas — NineStyle social integrations let you put them to work. By connecting Instagram, Pinterest, or TikTok, you can pull saved fashion content directly into your NineStyle inspiration board, map those looks to items in your existing wardrobe, and share polished outfit images back to your audience without ever leaving the app. Social integrations close the loop between discovering a look you love and actually wearing it.

## Supported platforms

NineStyle currently supports social integrations with the following platforms:

- **Instagram** — Import saved posts and Reels tagged as fashion; share outfit images to Stories.
- **Pinterest** — Sync style boards to your NineStyle inspiration board; pin outfits directly from the Outfit Builder.
- **TikTok** — Save fashion videos to your inspiration feed; share outfit videos to your TikTok profile.
- **NineStyle Community Feed** — Explore outfits shared publicly by other NineStyle members, follow stylists, and get featured in curated trend roundups — no external account needed.

## Connecting Instagram

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Profile">
    Select your avatar in the top-right corner of NineStyle to open your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Connected Apps">
    Select **Connected Apps** from the profile menu, then choose the **Social** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Instagram">
    Find Instagram in the list and select **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to Instagram">
    You are redirected to Instagram's authorization page. Enter your Instagram credentials if you are not already logged in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    Review the permissions NineStyle is requesting — read access to your saved posts and basic profile info — and select **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    You are returned to NineStyle. Instagram now shows as **Connected**. NineStyle begins scanning your saved posts for fashion-tagged content and adds matching items to your **Inspiration Board**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What NineStyle imports from Instagram**
NineStyle reads your saved posts and identifies those tagged or categorized as fashion using image recognition. Matching posts are added to your Inspiration Board, where you can pin individual items to outfit concepts or use them as references when building new looks in the Outfit Builder.

## Connecting Pinterest

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Profile">
    Select your avatar in the top-right corner of NineStyle to open your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Connected Apps">
    Select **Connected Apps** from the profile menu, then choose the **Social** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pinterest">
    Find Pinterest in the list and select **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to Pinterest">
    You are redirected to Pinterest's authorization page. Enter your Pinterest credentials if you are not already logged in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select boards to sync">
    After granting access, NineStyle displays a list of your Pinterest boards. Toggle on the boards you want to sync — for example, your "Street Style" or "Work Outfits" boards — and select **Save Selection**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    You are returned to NineStyle. Pins from your selected boards are imported into your Inspiration Board and refreshed automatically whenever you add new pins on Pinterest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sharing outfits to social media

Once you have built an outfit in the **Outfit Builder**, you can share it directly to your connected social accounts.

1. Open the outfit you want to share in the Outfit Builder.
2. Select **Share Outfit** in the top-right toolbar.
3. Choose your export format — square (1:1) for feed posts or vertical (9:16) for Stories and TikTok.
4. Select the platform you want to share to: **Instagram Stories**, **Pinterest Board**, or **TikTok**.
5. Add a caption or description in the sharing dialog, then select **Post**.

NineStyle generates a clean, branded outfit image using your wardrobe item photos and your chosen layout template. You can customize the background color, font style, and whether item labels appear on the image before posting.

<Note>
  NineStyle never publishes content to your social accounts automatically or without your explicit confirmation. Every share requires you to review the content and actively select **Post** in the sharing dialog. You remain in full control of what appears on your social profiles.
</Note>
