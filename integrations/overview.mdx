## How integrations work

NineStyle uses **OAuth 2.0** to connect to third-party apps and services. OAuth is an industry-standard authorization protocol that lets you grant NineStyle limited, specific access to your accounts — without ever sharing your passwords. When you initiate a connection, you are redirected to the third-party platform's own login and consent screen. You choose exactly what permissions to grant, and you can revoke access at any time. NineStyle stores only a secure access token on your behalf; your credentials never touch our servers.

## Managing connections

You can view, add, and remove all active integrations from a single location in your account.

1. Open your **Account** menu from the top-right corner of any NineStyle page.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Connected Apps**.
3. Find the integration you want to manage and select **Manage**.
4. To disconnect a service, select **Disconnect** and confirm. NineStyle will immediately revoke its access token and stop syncing data from that service.

Disconnecting an integration does not delete data already imported into your wardrobe. To remove imported items, go to your **Wardrobe** and delete them individually or in bulk.

<Note>
  When you authorize an integration, NineStyle requests only the minimum permissions required to deliver the feature. You can review exactly which permissions are active for each connection on the **Connected Apps** page. NineStyle never requests write access to your social accounts or retailer accounts beyond what is explicitly needed.
</Note>
