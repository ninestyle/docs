Setting up your NineStyle account properly from the start ensures that every feature — from AI outfit recommendations to personalized shopping suggestions — works with accurate context about you. This guide walks you through the full account setup process: creating your account, verifying your email, filling in your style profile, choosing a plan, and downloading the mobile app so you're ready to style on every device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for NineStyle">
    Go to [app.ninestyle.com/signup](https://app.ninestyle.com/signup) and enter your **email address** and a **password**. Your password must be at least 10 characters long and include a mix of letters and numbers.

    Once you submit the form, NineStyle creates your account instantly and sends a verification email to the address you provided. You can log in right away, but full access to AI features requires a verified email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Open the verification email from NineStyle and click **Verify my email**. This one-click step confirms your address and unlocks the full platform — including the Discover tab, AI recommendations, and outfit scheduling.

    <Note>
      Use a strong, unique password for your NineStyle account and consider enabling **two-factor authentication (2FA)** for an extra layer of security. You can turn on 2FA at any time under **Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**. NineStyle supports authenticator apps and SMS-based verification.
    </Note>

    If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. You can also request a new verification email from the login page by clicking **Resend verification email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your style profile">
    After verifying your email, NineStyle prompts you to fill in your style profile. This information powers every recommendation and outfit suggestion you receive, so be as accurate as you can.

    Provide the following details:

    - **Body measurements** — enter your height, weight, and key measurements (chest, waist, hips) so NineStyle can suggest items in the right sizes and cuts
    - **Style preferences** — choose the aesthetics that resonate with you, such as minimalist, business casual, streetwear, or bohemian; you can select multiple
    - **Budget range** — set a monthly or per-item budget ceiling so shopping recommendations stay within your comfort zone

    Select **Save profile** when you're done. You can update any of these fields later from **Account Settings → Style Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    NineStyle offers two plans designed for different levels of engagement. Review the options and select the one that fits your needs.

    | Feature | Free | Pro |
    |---|---|---|
    | Wardrobe items | Up to 50 | Unlimited |
    | AI recommendations | 3 per week | Unlimited daily |
    | Outfit builder | Basic | Advanced (with scheduling) |
    | Shopping suggestions | — | Included |
    | Priority support | — | Included |

    Select **Free** to get started at no cost, or choose **Pro** and enter your billing details to unlock the full experience. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from [Account Settings → Plans](/account/plans).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the mobile app">
    NineStyle's mobile app lets you manage your wardrobe, browse outfit recommendations, and plan looks wherever you are. Download it from your device's app store and log in with the same credentials you created above.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="iOS">
        Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad and search for **NineStyle**. Tap **Get** to install, then open the app and sign in with your email and password.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Android">
        Open **Google Play** on your Android device and search for **NineStyle**. Tap **Install**, then open the app and sign in with your email and password.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Updating your profile

You can revisit and change any of your account settings at any time. On the web app, click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings**. On mobile, tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom navigation bar, then tap the gear icon.

From Account Settings you can update your:

- **Personal details** — name, email address, and profile photo
- **Style profile** — body measurements, preferred aesthetics, and budget range
- **Notifications** — choose which alerts you receive by email and push notification
- **Privacy** — control what data NineStyle uses and manage third-party connections
- **Security** — update your password and manage two-factor authentication
- **Plan and billing** — upgrade to Pro, view invoices, or cancel your subscription

Changes to your style profile take effect immediately and influence the next round of AI recommendations generated for you.

<Note>
  Keep your password secure and never share your NineStyle credentials. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, go to **Account Settings → Security** and select **Sign out of all devices**, then change your password immediately. You can also reach NineStyle support at support@ninestyle.com.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Connect your Instagram or Pinterest account under **Account Settings → Connected Apps** to give NineStyle's AI a richer picture of your style. The more visual style signals the engine has, the more precise and varied your daily outfit recommendations will be.
</Tip>
