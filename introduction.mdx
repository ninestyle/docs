NineStyle is a personal styling and wardrobe management platform designed for anyone who wants to take the guesswork out of getting dressed. By combining a digital wardrobe catalogue with an AI recommendation engine and a flexible outfit builder, NineStyle learns your taste over time and surfaces looks that actually work for your lifestyle, body type, and color preferences. Whether you're a fashion enthusiast with hundreds of pieces or someone who simply wants to get more value from the clothes they already own, NineStyle meets you where you are and helps you build a style that feels genuinely yours.

## How NineStyle works

NineStyle's experience is built around a simple three-step loop that gets smarter the more you use it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your wardrobe">
    Start by adding your clothes to NineStyle. Upload photos directly from your phone or desktop, or search the catalog to find items you own. Each item is automatically tagged with color, category, and fabric type, and you can add your own labels to keep things organized the way you prefer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your style">
    Complete your style profile by selecting the aesthetics that resonate with you — minimalist, streetwear, business casual, and more. Add your body measurements and set a budget range for any new pieces you'd consider buying. NineStyle uses all of this context to calibrate its recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discover and refine">
    Browse AI-generated outfit recommendations on the Discover tab every day. Like or save looks you love, and dismiss ones that don't fit. Every interaction trains the recommendation engine, so your suggestions get more accurate and personal over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
