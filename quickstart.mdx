This quickstart guide walks you through the fastest path from a brand-new account to your first personalized outfit recommendation. By the end, you'll have a NineStyle account, a style profile that reflects your taste, at least one wardrobe item ready to style, and a live recommendation waiting for you on the Discover tab. The whole process takes under five minutes — so let's get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to [app.ninestyle.com/signup](https://app.ninestyle.com/signup) and fill in your email address and a secure password. After you submit the form, NineStyle sends a verification email to the address you provided.

    Open the email and click **Verify my email** to activate your account. If the email doesn't arrive within a minute or two, check your spam folder or use the **Resend verification email** link on the confirmation page.

    <Note>
      Your account is created immediately, but some features — including AI recommendations — are locked until your email is verified. Verification takes just one click.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your style profile">
    After verification, NineStyle walks you through a short onboarding survey. Work through each screen to define:

    - **Body type** — choose the silhouette that best matches your proportions to help the AI suggest flattering fits
    - **Preferred styles** — select one or more aesthetics (e.g., minimalist, classic, streetwear, bohemian) that reflect how you like to dress
    - **Color palette** — pick the hues you gravitate toward and any colors you prefer to avoid

    Select **Save profile** when you're done. You can revisit and update any of these preferences at any time from your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first items">
    Head to the **Wardrobe** tab and add your first pieces using one of two methods:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Upload a photo">
        Tap **Add item → Upload photo** and select an image from your device. NineStyle automatically detects the garment type, dominant color, and suggested category. Confirm or adjust the tags, then tap **Save item**.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Browse the catalog">
        Tap **Add item → Browse catalog** and search by brand, style, or category. When you find a match, tap **Add to wardrobe** to import the item's details — including high-quality images — directly into your closet.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    For the best recommendations, tag each item with its **category** (e.g., tops, trousers, footwear), **color**, and **brand**. The more context each item carries, the more accurate your outfit suggestions will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your first recommendation">
    With your style profile set and at least one item in your wardrobe, you're ready for your first recommendation. Tap **Get Recommendations** on the home screen, or navigate directly to the **Discover** tab.

    NineStyle's AI engine analyses your wardrobe, style preferences, and — if you've granted access — your local weather and upcoming calendar events to generate outfit suggestions tailored to you. Like any outfit to save it; dismiss suggestions you don't want to see again.

    <Tip>
      The more items you add to your wardrobe before visiting Discover, the richer and more varied your initial recommendations will be. Aim for at least five items across different categories to see full outfit suggestions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All of the steps above are available in the NineStyle mobile app as well. Download **NineStyle** from the App Store or Google Play to manage your wardrobe and browse recommendations from your phone.
</Note>
