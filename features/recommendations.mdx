NineStyle's recommendation engine works in the background, learning from every outfit you rate, every item you add to your wardrobe, and every preference you set in your style profile. The result is a daily feed of outfit ideas and shopping picks that gets more accurate the more you use the app — not generic suggestions scraped from a trend report, but combinations and products chosen specifically because they match your aesthetic, fit your budget, and work with what you already own.

## Daily outfit picks

Your personalized outfit recommendations are refreshed every morning and live in the **Discover** tab. Each pick is drawn from items already in your wardrobe, assembled by the AI into complete looks based on your style profile and recent activity.

### Finding your daily picks

Open the **Discover** tab from the bottom navigation bar. The top section, labeled **Today's Picks**, displays your daily outfit recommendations. Scroll down to see additional picks for different occasions and moods. Tap any outfit card to expand it and see the full item breakdown, including individual wardrobe pieces used.

### Training the AI with likes and dislikes

The fastest way to improve your recommendations is to give the AI explicit feedback:

- Tap the **heart icon** on an outfit card to like it — NineStyle registers what works and surfaces more combinations with similar attributes.
- Tap the **X icon** to dismiss a pick — the AI deprioritizes those patterns in future suggestions.
- Rate outfits you actually wear (from the Outfit Builder or your Outfits library) to give the AI your most accurate signal.

The more consistently you react to suggestions, the faster your Discover feed aligns with your taste.

### Weather-aware recommendations

NineStyle uses your device's location (when permitted) to check the day's forecast and filter recommendations accordingly. If it's below 10°C, the engine prioritizes outerwear-inclusive looks and heavier fabrics. On warm days, it surfaces lighter, seasonally appropriate combinations. Toggle this feature on or off under **Settings → Recommendations → Weather-Aware Picks**.

## Shopping recommendations

Beyond your existing wardrobe, NineStyle identifies gaps and surfaces new items worth adding — from connected retailers that match your budget and aesthetic.

### Wardrobe gap analysis

NineStyle periodically scans your wardrobe for missing building blocks — the pieces that would unlock the most new outfit combinations. Common gap flags include:

- A neutral layering piece that would pair with many existing tops
- A category where you have very few options (e.g., only one pair of shoes)
- Seasonal items you're missing ahead of the next weather change

Shopping recommendations based on gap analysis appear in the **Discover** tab under **Add to Your Wardrobe**.

### Price range filters

Every shopping recommendation respects the budget range you set in your style profile. If you want to tighten or expand the range temporarily, tap **Filter** on the shopping section and adjust the price slider — your changes apply immediately without updating your long-term profile settings.

### Connected retailers

NineStyle partners with a curated set of retailers to surface shoppable items directly in the app. Tap any shopping recommendation to open the product page and complete your purchase without leaving NineStyle. For the full list of connected stores and how to manage retailer preferences, see the [Shopping Integrations](/integrations/shopping) page.

### Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Why am I seeing items I don't own?">
  Shopping recommendations intentionally surface items you don't yet own — they're suggestions for what to buy, not outfit ideas drawn from your existing wardrobe. If you're seeing these in the **Today's Picks** outfit section (rather than the shopping section), it means those items appeared in a look you saved from the community feed or an inspiration board. You can add them to a wishlist or dismiss them using the **X** button.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I remove a suggestion?">
  Tap the **X** icon on any outfit or shopping card to dismiss it from your feed. For shopping items, you can also tap **Not Interested** on the expanded product view to prevent that specific item or similar products from appearing again. If you want to clear your entire dismissal history and start fresh, go to **Settings → Recommendations → Reset Dismissed Items**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I reset my recommendations?">
  Yes. Go to **Settings → Recommendations → Reset Recommendation History**. This clears all your likes, dislikes, and outfit ratings and returns the engine to its defaults — your style profile and wardrobe data are preserved, but the AI starts re-learning your preferences from scratch. Use this if your feed has drifted significantly from what you actually want to see.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Activate a **Style Mood** before browsing your recommendations to get occasion-specific outfit picks without permanently changing your style profile. If you have a dinner out tonight or a job interview tomorrow morning, switching to the relevant mood instantly reshapes your Discover feed to surface appropriate looks. See [Style Moods](/features/style-profiles#style-moods) for the full list of available moods and how to activate them.
</Tip>
