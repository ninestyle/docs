Your style profile is the engine behind every recommendation NineStyle makes. Without it, the AI is working in the dark — with it, every outfit suggestion, shopping pick, and daily look is calibrated to your actual taste, your body, and your life. Setting up your profile takes about five minutes, and refining it over time is one of the most effective things you can do to make NineStyle feel like it truly knows you.

## What is a style profile?

A style profile is a structured snapshot of your personal style preferences. NineStyle uses it alongside your wardrobe data and behavior to generate recommendations that fit the way you actually want to dress. Your profile is built across four core dimensions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Aesthetic" icon="palette" href="/features/style-profiles#setting-up-your-style-profile">
    Choose the visual styles that resonate with you — **Minimal**, **Bohemian**, **Classic**, **Streetwear**, **Preppy**, **Romantic**, **Edgy**, and more. You can select multiple aesthetics to reflect a blended personal style.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Color Palette" icon="swatchbook" href="/features/style-profiles#setting-up-your-style-profile">
    Define your preferred color families — neutrals, earth tones, pastels, bold primaries, or monochromes. NineStyle uses your palette to filter both outfit suggestions and shopping picks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Body Shape" icon="person" href="/features/style-profiles#setting-up-your-style-profile">
    Select your body shape to help NineStyle prioritize silhouettes and fits that are most flattering for you. Options include Hourglass, Pear, Apple, Rectangle, and Inverted Triangle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Budget Range" icon="circle-dollar-sign" href="/features/style-profiles#setting-up-your-style-profile">
    Set your typical per-item spending range. NineStyle uses this to calibrate shopping recommendations so it never surfaces items that are consistently outside what you want to spend.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setting up your style profile

If you skipped the onboarding flow, you can complete or update your style profile at any time from your account settings. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Profile">
    Tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom navigation bar to open your account page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Style Profile">
    Tap **Style Profile** to enter the profile editor. You'll see your current progress across each dimension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your Aesthetic">
    Browse the aesthetic tiles and tap every style that resonates with you. Don't overthink it — you can always change these later. Tap **Next** when you're done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your Color Palette">
    Select the color families you gravitate toward in your wardrobe and shopping. Swipe through the palette grid and tap to toggle colors on or off. Tap **Next** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your Body Shape">
    Select the body shape option that best describes you. Each option includes a brief description of the silhouette to help you choose. Tap **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your Budget Range">
    Use the sliders to set a comfortable per-item minimum and maximum spend. You can set separate ranges for different categories (e.g., a higher range for shoes, lower for basics). Tap **Save** to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Select **multiple aesthetics** — most people's style is a blend. If you lean Minimal but love the occasional Bohemian piece, selecting both teaches the AI to serve that range rather than locking you into a single box. NineStyle weights aesthetics by how frequently you engage with them, so your feed naturally shifts over time.
</Tip>

## Style moods

Your style profile represents your baseline preferences, but your actual dressing needs change from day to day. **Style Moods** are temporary overrides that shift the lens of your recommendations for a specific moment — without touching your long-term profile.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Available moods">
    NineStyle includes a set of built-in moods you can activate at any time:

    | Mood | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Going Out Tonight** | Evening events, dinners, parties |
    | **Work from Home** | Comfortable but put-together looks |
    | **Office Day** | Professional, polished outfits |
    | **Weekend Casual** | Relaxed everyday dressing |
    | **Active & Outdoors** | Sporty and functional pieces |
    | **Special Occasion** | Formalwear and dressed-up looks |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="How to activate a mood">
    To activate a Style Mood:

    1. Open the **Discover** tab.
    2. Tap the **Mood** icon (the smiley face) at the top of the screen.
    3. Select a mood from the list.
    4. Tap **Apply** — your recommendations refresh instantly.

    Moods expire after 24 hours by default. You can manually clear a mood at any time by returning to the Mood picker and tapping **Reset to Default**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Updating your profile

Your style evolves — NineStyle is designed to evolve with it. Revisiting your style profile every few months (or after a big wardrobe refresh) keeps your recommendations sharp and relevant.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="When to update" icon="rotate" href="/features/style-profiles#updating-your-profile">
    Update your profile after a significant life change (new job, new climate, new season), after a major wardrobe overhaul, or whenever your recommendations start to feel slightly off.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Why it matters" icon="brain" href="/features/style-profiles#updating-your-profile">
    Each profile update gives the recommendation engine a recalibrated baseline. Combined with your ongoing outfit ratings, updates compound over time — the longer you use NineStyle and keep your profile current, the more accurate it becomes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
