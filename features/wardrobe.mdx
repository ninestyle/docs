Your digital wardrobe is the foundation of everything NineStyle does for you. Instead of staring into a physical closet and drawing a blank, your digital wardrobe gives you a searchable, visual inventory of every clothing item you own — tagged by category, color, brand, and season. The more complete your wardrobe, the smarter your outfit recommendations, the more accurate your style analytics, and the easier it becomes to get dressed every single day.

## Adding items to your wardrobe

Every great wardrobe starts with getting your clothes into NineStyle. You can add items one at a time or import them in bulk — either way, the process takes only a few seconds per piece.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Wardrobe tab">
    Tap **Wardrobe** in the bottom navigation bar to open your wardrobe dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add Item">
    Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner to start adding a new clothing item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a photo or use the camera">
    Choose **Take Photo** to snap a picture of the item directly, or tap **Upload from Library** to select an existing image from your device. NineStyle automatically removes the background so your item appears cleanly against a neutral backdrop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the item details">
    Complete the item card with the following fields:
    - **Name** — a short label (e.g., "White Oxford Shirt")
    - **Category** — Tops, Bottoms, Shoes, Accessories, or Outerwear
    - **Color** — pick from the color picker or let NineStyle auto-detect
    - **Brand** — type or search the brand name
    - **Season** — Spring/Summer, Fall/Winter, or All-Season
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the item">
    Tap **Save** to add the item to your wardrobe. It will appear immediately in your wardrobe grid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Speed up the process with **Bulk Import**. Go to **Wardrobe → Import → Bulk Import**, then select multiple photos from your camera roll at once. NineStyle processes each image, pre-fills detected details, and queues them for your review — ideal when you're adding a full season's wardrobe in one sitting.
</Tip>

## Organizing your wardrobe

NineStyle keeps your wardrobe structured out of the box and gives you the flexibility to organize further in any way that fits how you think about your clothes.

### Categories

Every item belongs to one of five default categories:

  <Card title="Tops" icon="shirt" href="/features/wardrobe#categories">
    T-shirts, blouses, sweaters, hoodies, and anything worn on the upper body.

  <Card title="Bottoms" icon="scissors" href="/features/wardrobe#categories">
    Jeans, trousers, skirts, shorts, and all lower-body garments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shoes" icon="shoe-prints" href="/features/wardrobe#categories">
    Sneakers, boots, heels, sandals, and every pair of footwear you own.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accessories" icon="gem" href="/features/wardrobe#categories">
    Bags, hats, belts, jewelry, scarves, and other finishing touches.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outerwear" icon="cloud-sun" href="/features/wardrobe#categories">
    Coats, jackets, blazers, and anything worn as a top layer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Filtering and sorting

Use the **Filter** button at the top of your wardrobe grid to narrow your view by:

- **Category** — show only one type of item at a time
- **Color** — surface everything in a specific color family
- **Season** — see what's appropriate for the current or upcoming season
- **Brand** — browse all items from a particular label
- **Tags** — filter by any custom tag you've applied

Sort your wardrobe by **Recently Added**, **Most Worn**, **Least Worn**, or **A–Z** to surface the right items at the right moment.

### Creating custom collections

Collections let you group items that don't neatly fit a single category — think "Weekend Hiking Gear," "Work Capsule," or "Holiday Party Options." To create one, go to **Wardrobe → Collections → New Collection**, give it a name, and drag items into it. Items can belong to multiple collections simultaneously, and collections sync across all your devices.

## Wardrobe analytics

NineStyle tracks how you actually use your wardrobe and surfaces insights that help you shop smarter and dress better.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cost-Per-Wear" icon="chart-line" href="/features/wardrobe#wardrobe-analytics">
    Every time you log an outfit that includes an item, NineStyle divides the item's purchase price by its total wear count. Items with a high cost-per-wear are candidates for more use — or for removal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Most & Never Worn" icon="star" href="/features/wardrobe#wardrobe-analytics">
    The **Most Worn** view ranks your go-to pieces so you can identify the core of your personal style. The **Never Worn** view surfaces items that haven't made it into a single outfit — a useful prompt to either style them or donate them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Season Readiness" icon="calendar" href="/features/wardrobe#wardrobe-analytics">
    Before a new season starts, NineStyle generates a **Season Readiness Report** that flags which seasonal categories are well-stocked and which have gaps — so you know exactly what to shop for before the temperature changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wardrobe Value" icon="tag" href="/features/wardrobe#wardrobe-analytics">
    See the estimated total value of your wardrobe, broken down by category, to understand where your clothing budget is concentrated.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
