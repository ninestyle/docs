The NineStyle Outfit Builder gives you a visual canvas where you can combine items from your wardrobe into complete looks — before you ever touch a hanger. Drag pieces together, layer them, preview how they work as a set, and save the results as named outfits you can pull up on any morning. Whether you're planning a week of work looks or putting together a special occasion outfit, the builder turns your wardrobe into a creative tool rather than a daily source of stress.

## Building an outfit

Creating a new outfit takes less than a minute once your wardrobe is populated. Follow these steps to put your first look together:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Outfits tab">
    Tap **Outfits** in the bottom navigation bar to go to your saved looks and the builder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap New Outfit">
    Tap the **+ New Outfit** button at the top of the screen to open a blank builder canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a base item">
    Start with an anchor piece — typically a top, a dress, or a statement bottom. Tap **Add Item**, browse your wardrobe, and select the piece you want to build around. It appears on the canvas as the first layer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add complementary pieces">
    Tap **Add Item** again to layer in additional pieces. NineStyle surfaces smart suggestions (see below) as you add each item, or you can browse your full wardrobe manually. Build out the look with bottoms, shoes, and accessories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the layers">
    Tap and hold any item on the canvas to reorder layers, resize, or reposition it. This is especially useful for outerwear and accessories where visual overlap matters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and save the outfit">
    Tap the **Name** field at the top of the canvas and give your outfit a title (e.g., "Tuesday Meeting" or "Saturday Brunch"). Tap **Save** — the outfit is added to your Outfits library and immediately available in the Outfit Calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using smart suggestions

You don't have to browse your entire wardrobe to find the right pairing. NineStyle's smart suggestion engine analyzes your existing wardrobe items and surfaces the ones most likely to work well with whatever you've already placed on the canvas.

**How suggestions work:** As soon as you add your first item, NineStyle evaluates color harmony, style aesthetic, category balance, and season against every other item in your wardrobe. It then displays a ranked shortlist of suggested additions at the bottom of the builder screen — tap any suggestion to add it to the canvas instantly.

### Complete the look

When your outfit has two or more items, tap **Complete the Look** to let NineStyle fill in any missing category slots automatically. If you have a top and bottom on the canvas but no shoes or bag, NineStyle selects the highest-compatibility options from your wardrobe and adds them as a suggested completion. You can accept all suggestions at once or swap individual pieces before saving.

## Outfit calendar

Plan what you're going to wear before the week begins. The Outfit Calendar lets you assign saved outfits to specific dates so you're never making last-minute decisions when you're already running late.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Scheduling outfits">
    To schedule an outfit:

    1. Go to **Outfits → Calendar**.
    2. Tap the date you want to plan for.
    3. Tap **Assign Outfit** and select a saved look from your library.
    4. Optionally, add a note (e.g., "Client presentation" or "Date night").
    5. Tap **Confirm** — the outfit appears on that date in your calendar view.

    You can schedule outfits up to 90 days in advance and assign multiple looks to a single day (morning, afternoon, and evening slots are available).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Calendar sync">
    Connect NineStyle to your device calendar so that your scheduled outfits appear alongside your existing events — and so NineStyle can use your event names to suggest appropriate looks.

    **To sync your calendar:**
    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Calendar**.
    2. Tap **Connect** next to **iOS Calendar** or **Google Calendar**.
    3. Grant the required permissions when prompted.
    4. Toggle on **Show outfits in calendar app** to have scheduled looks appear as calendar events on your device.

    <Warning>
      NineStyle only reads event titles and dates — it never accesses event descriptions, attendees, or any other calendar data.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sharing outfits

Your saved looks don't have to stay private. NineStyle makes it easy to share outfits with the community or export them for your own social media channels.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Share to Community Feed" icon="users" href="/features/outfit-builder#sharing-outfits">
    Tap **Share → NineStyle Community** on any saved outfit to post it to the public feed. Other NineStyle users can like, comment, and save your look to their own inspiration boards. Community sharing is the best way to discover how others style similar pieces.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Export as Image" icon="image" href="/features/outfit-builder#sharing-outfits">
    Tap **Share → Export Image** to generate a clean, styled flat-lay image of your outfit. Choose from square (for Instagram), vertical (for Stories/TikTok), or landscape formats. The exported image includes your outfit name and a NineStyle watermark that you can toggle off on Pro and Premium plans.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Every outfit you save can be rated on a scale of 1 to 5 stars after you've worn it. Your ratings are a direct input to NineStyle's recommendation engine — outfits you rate highly inform future AI outfit picks and help the system understand which combinations genuinely work for you. Rate your outfits from **Outfits → [outfit name] → Rate This Look**.
</Note>
