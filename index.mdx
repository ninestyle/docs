## Get up and running in four steps

Starting with NineStyle is straightforward. Follow the onboarding flow below and you'll have a fully personalized styling experience ready in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.ninestyle.com/signup](https://app.ninestyle.com/signup) with your email address and a secure password. Verify your email to activate your account and unlock all features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your wardrobe">
    Add items to your virtual wardrobe by uploading photos of your existing clothing or browsing the NineStyle catalog. Tag each item with its category, color, and brand for the best results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your style profile">
    Tell NineStyle about your body type, preferred styles, color palette, and budget range. The more detail you provide, the more accurate and relevant your recommendations will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get recommendations">
    Head to the **Discover** tab or tap **Get Recommendations** to receive your first AI-powered outfit suggestions, curated specifically for your wardrobe and style preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  NineStyle is available on iOS and Android. Download the mobile app to manage your wardrobe, browse outfit recommendations, and plan looks on the go. Search for **NineStyle** in the App Store or Google Play.
</Note>
