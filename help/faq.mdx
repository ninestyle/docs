## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is NineStyle free to use?">
    Yes. NineStyle offers a **Free plan** that gives you access to core wardrobe and outfit features at no cost. If you want unlimited wardrobe items, advanced AI recommendations, and priority support, upgrade to **Pro for $9.99/month**. You can upgrade at any time from **Account → Billing**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms is NineStyle available on?">
    NineStyle is available on the following platforms:

    - **Web** — access NineStyle from any modern browser at [app.ninestyle.com](https://app.ninestyle.com)
    - **iOS** — download the app from the Apple App Store
    - **Android** — download the app from the Google Play Store

    Your account and wardrobe sync automatically across all platforms, so you can switch between devices without losing anything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to upload photos of all my clothes?">
    No. Uploading photos is optional. You can build your wardrobe in three ways:

    1. **Upload a photo** of an item you already own
    2. **Search the NineStyle catalog** and add matching items directly
    3. **Add items manually** by entering details like category, color, and brand

    Many users find a combination of all three methods works best, using photos for key pieces and catalog search for everyday basics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Wardrobe & Outfits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many items can I add to my wardrobe?">
    The number of wardrobe items you can add depends on your plan:

    | Plan | Item Limit |
    |------|------------|
    | Free | 50 items |
    | Pro  | Unlimited  |

    If you reach the Free plan limit, you'll need to remove existing items or upgrade to Pro before adding new ones.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I organize clothes into collections?">
    Yes. NineStyle supports **custom collections**, which let you group items however you like — by season, occasion, travel capsule, or any other category you choose. To create a collection, navigate to **Wardrobe → Collections → New Collection**, give it a name, and start adding items.

    <Tip>
      Collections also improve the quality of your outfit recommendations by giving the AI more context about how you like to group your clothing.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete an item from my wardrobe?">
    The steps differ slightly depending on which platform you're using:

    - **Mobile (iOS & Android):** Long-press the item card, then tap **Delete** in the menu that appears.
    - **Web:** Right-click the item card, then select **Delete** from the context menu.

    <Warning>
      Deleting an item is permanent and cannot be undone. If you no longer wear something but want to keep it in your history, consider archiving it instead.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recommendations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does NineStyle generate outfit recommendations?">
    NineStyle's AI engine builds outfit suggestions by analyzing multiple signals at once:

    - **Your wardrobe** — the items, colors, and styles you own
    - **Your style profile** — preferences you set during onboarding or update in **Profile → Style Settings**
    - **Weather** — current and forecast conditions based on your location
    - **Your calendar** — upcoming events that inform occasion-appropriate suggestions

    The more data you provide — by rating outfits, updating your wardrobe, and keeping your calendar connected — the more accurate your recommendations become over time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why are my recommendations not improving?">
    If your recommendations feel stale or off-target, try these steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Rate more outfits">
        Open **Discover → Outfit Feed** and use the thumbs-up/thumbs-down buttons. The AI learns directly from your ratings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Keep your wardrobe current">
        Remove items you no longer own and add recent additions. Outdated wardrobe data leads to outdated suggestions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Refine your style profile">
        Go to **Profile → Style Settings** and revisit your aesthetic preferences, color palette, and fit preferences.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get occasion-specific recommendations?">
    Yes. Use **Style Moods** to tell NineStyle what kind of outfit you need. Style Moods include options like *Work*, *Weekend*, *Date Night*, *Gym*, and *Travel*, among others. Select a mood from the **Discover** tab, and NineStyle will filter recommendations to suit that occasion. You can also combine a Style Mood with a specific date on your calendar for even more tailored results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my Pro subscription?">
    To cancel your Pro subscription, follow these steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Account settings">
        Tap or click your profile avatar, then select **Account**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
        Select **Billing** from the account menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Cancel your subscription">
        Click **Cancel Subscription** and confirm when prompted.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Your Pro features remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I lose my data if I downgrade to Free?">
    No. Your wardrobe data is never deleted when you downgrade. However, if your wardrobe contains more than 50 items, everything beyond that limit becomes **read-only** — you can still view those items but cannot edit or delete them until you're back within the 50-item limit or upgrade to Pro again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use NineStyle on multiple devices?">
    Yes. Your NineStyle account syncs automatically across all devices — web, iOS, and Android. Simply sign in with the same email address and password, and your wardrobe, collections, style profile, and preferences will be available everywhere.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my wardrobe?">
    Only you can see your wardrobe by default. NineStyle does not share your wardrobe contents with other users or third parties without your explicit consent. If you choose to share a collection or outfit via a public link in the future, you control exactly what is visible. You can review and adjust all sharing preferences in **Settings → Privacy**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my wardrobe data?">
    Yes. You can export a full copy of your wardrobe data at any time in either **JSON** or **CSV** format. To export, go to **Settings → Privacy → Export My Data**, choose your preferred format, and a download link will be emailed to your registered address within a few minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    To permanently delete your NineStyle account, go to **Settings → Privacy → Delete Account** and follow the on-screen confirmation steps.

    <Warning>
      Account deletion is **irreversible**. All your wardrobe data, style profile, outfit history, and billing information will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered. If you're unsure, consider exporting your data first.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
